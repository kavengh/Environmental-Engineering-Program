The Environmental Engineering & Technology Program is a four- year program designed to provide students entry-level skills and a foundational understanding of the world of Engineering and Sustainability. The program’s vision is to provide every student access to academic knowledge and basic technical skills required to be successful in pursuing engineering and STEM careers, by combining engineering, mathematics, and science courses that result in a logical thought process and problem-solving skills. Students will develop essential career preparation capabilities through technical experience, academic courses, work-based learning and internship opportunities. Employment of engineers and STEM related careers is expected to grow over the next decade.

Program’s Goals and Curricular

  • Increase the number of young people, without regard to gender or ethnic origin, who pursue engineering, technology and sustainability related careers.
  • Provide a relevant program that help prepare students for a highly skilled working environment.
  • To develop in students career and college readiness awareness and achievement.
  • To continue to offer meaningful WBL opportunities that enhance and connect the classroom curriculum to the workplace.
